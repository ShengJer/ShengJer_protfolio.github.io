Welcome to my personal academic website.

I am **Sheng-Jer Chen**, a Ph.D. candidate in Chemical Engineering at National Taiwan University and a project research assistant at the National Center for High-Performance Computing (NCHC). My work sits at the intersection of scientific computing, machine learning, and polymer physics.

## Research Interests

- High-performance computing for scientific simulation
- Polymer field theory and Cahn-Hilliard-type phase evolution
- Physics-informed and data-driven modeling
- Low-dimensional representation and surrogate modeling for complex systems
